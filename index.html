About Us

Fricanee is Africa and nee all at once, Frica-nee. It means originate from Africa. We value sustainability, wearability, youthfullness and timeless. All of our pieces are lovingly crafted to voice these themes. We currently ethically produce and operate from Accra and Cape Coast, Ghana. African style is far from colloquial, it's as expensive, diverse and intricate as the melanin variations of our skin. Our fashion wavelength sends out vibrations like the sounds of our indigenous drums, untamed and dictated by the trends we set at our own evolving pace. Fricanee creations resonate with the one who values evolving, forward fashion owns their style and leans towards mild statement pieces.

FAQs

All Fricanee garments are carefully constructed to give you the perfect fit. If for any reason however, you would like to make an exchange or get refund, you have 14 days from the day of purchase to send it back to us.

For returns to be accepted, garments must:
  • Be in new condition.
  • Be unworn.
  • Be unwashed.
  • Be unaltered.
  • Be of stains, odours and damages.

To process a return, kindly follow the guidelines below:
  1. Send an email to specify if you would like an exchange or refund. (If you would like a refund, kindly include the account details you would like to receive the refund in the email).
  2. Exchanges are delivered within 5 working days of receiving initial garments. Refunds are precessed within 7 working days of your request.
